How to play: Press and release your spacebar to jump. When your tile land on a floor with number, your tile merges its number!

Note: This site is actually the official version of Jump65536, and TheReal96's is not. You can find the original 65536 via http://yudongqiu.github.io/65536.

Not a ripoff of 2048 by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
